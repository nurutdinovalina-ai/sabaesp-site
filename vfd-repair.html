Clients typically contact us for oilfield VFD repair, ESP drive troubleshooting, variable frequency drive diagnostics, and fast recovery after trips or faults.

What we repair

We service and restore VFD/VSD units used in ESP installations across multiple OEMs (including major oilfield suppliers and industrial drive platforms).

Business impact: repairing existing VFDs often reduces lead time and can provide significant savings compared to replacement.

Your current website already highlights multi-OEM capability and engineering discipline — we keep that positioning consistent.

Typical work packages

  • Incoming inspection + fault isolation
  • Power stage diagnostics (IGBT / power modules)
  • Control/logic validation (MCU/DSP where applicable)
  • Firmware restoration and parameter recovery
  • Protection circuit verification
  • Cooling/thermal system check
  • Communication recovery (Fieldbus/Modbus/Profibus where applicable)
  • Load testing / functional verification

Diagnostics that clients value

  • Under-load verification where feasible (not only “power-on” checks)
  • Waveform / harmonics review when relevant
  • Clear “pass/fail” criteria and documented protections
  • Root-cause analysis (RCA) with preventive actions

When to contact us

  • Drive trips, unstable operation, nuisance alarms
  • Overcurrent / overvoltage / ground faults
  • Communication loss with surface control / SCADA
  • Thermal issues / cooling failures
  • Need for fast recovery to restore production

We can support remotely or coordinate onsite assistance depending on location and urgency.

How a repair project starts

  1. Send drive model + symptoms (or photos of alarms).
  2. We confirm feasibility, scope and timeline.
  3. Unit arrives for diagnostics (or we coordinate onsite support).
  4. You receive a repair report and quote before major work.
  5. Post-repair verification + documentation.
