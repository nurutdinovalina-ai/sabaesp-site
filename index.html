Advanced VFD Engineering, Repair & Diagnostics

We service, diagnose, and restore VFDs from multiple OEMs, including Schlumberger, Baker Hughes, Triol, Novomet, Borets, Toshiba, and other major OEMs.

Cost & lead time advantage: Restoring an existing VFD can deliver up to 70% cost savings and significantly reduce lead time compared to procuring new units.

Core capabilities

  • Power electronics & HV diagnostics (IGBT / power modules under load)
  • Firmware restoration, MCU/DSP programming, logic & protection validation
  • Fieldbus/Modbus/Profibus communication recovery
  • Cooling/thermal management and EMI/RFI mitigation
  • Load simulation testing and waveform/harmonics analysis
  • PCB-level repair and component replacement (where feasible)
  • Failure analysis (RCA) & preventive action reporting
